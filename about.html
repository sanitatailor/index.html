Our Story

Seoni Clothing was established in 1974 and is a family run organisation that has grown from strength to strength over the years. As an independent menswear fashion retailer, Seoni Clothing take great care in offering the best one to one customer service along with a large range of the latest trends from casual wear (polo tops, jeans, shirts) to smart wear (suits, ties, trousers). We also offer free alteration service on any garments that have been purchased from the store.
